My motivation for ICT came with the feeling working around the Computer Science. After school I started my career in telecommunications company as an IT Customer Support. In that company I received 4 months of training on telecommunications subject, later on I started working. At first I enjoyed every minute in my work, but later on I felt stuck as I reached learning boundary.. So I knew that I want to keep going and learn more and more to open my possibilities. In HZ University I can enjoy my new learning possibilities, also meeting new friends and colleagues with who I can learn and apply new skills together.
